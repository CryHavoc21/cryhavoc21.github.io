A note on 4x6" prints

For technical reasons, some of my 4x6" prints are not exactly 4 inches by 6 inches: they are slightly shorter on the longer side. This means the final size is closer to 4" by 5.75". Also, the photos are printed on varieties of glossy paper, but may not all be printed on the same paper stocks.

How to buy prints:

  1. Navigate to my PayPal seller page https://paypal.me/pigeonfilm.
  2. Click "Pay".
  3. Fill out the value with the cost amount from the bundle you want. Make sure you set the currency to USD
  4. Fill out the "What's this for?" box with the following info:
    • Which bundle you want to buy
    • Your mailing address
    • Your email
    • If you want any specific photos from the "Photos available" section
  5. Click "Pay". I will receive a notification of your payment and will send you confirmation of your order as soon as I can.

Shipping details

I will be shipping your photos via USPS untracked standard post aka "First-class mail", which should be delivered in the US in 1-5 business days. I can also send internationally to these countries, but delivery times may vary.

Shipping cost is included in the listed price
